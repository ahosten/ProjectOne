Akeda M. Hosten was born in Richmond, VA on June 27, 1994. He moved to Silver Spring, MD with his family in 1997. Akeda began taking piano lessons at the age of six and at the age of eight Akeda began writing his own rap music, performing for his family members and friends. In high school, Akeda began learning how to play guitar as well as learning how to record his own music. He began to perform at his schools talent shows and released his first CD. His senior year of high school Akeda released another CD titled Commencement Day. One of the songs on this CD, turn up the drums, was chosen to be featured in a Sig water bottle commercial. Akeda continued his music career in college, writing acoustic music as well as finishing another CD titled The Prodigy, which is scheduled to be released in the winter.
