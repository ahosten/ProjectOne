We Made It

We Made It is a freestyle over Drake and Soulja Boy's hit of the same title. On this track Akeda delivers a versatile flow complimented by brilliant punchlines and smooth alliteration.

Gone to the Well

Gone to the Well is one of Akeda's most recent songs off of his upcoming CD The Prodigy. It is a somewhat darker track with a unique instrumental beat produced by PJE.

OMG

OMG is the opening track on Akeda's upcoming CD The Prodigy. PJE provides another marvelous beat on this track, and Akeda takes full advantage.

Commencement Day

The title track from Akeda's previous CD Commencement Day. This song was written and recorded by Akeda in his high school's make shift recording studio in the last semester of his senior year.

Do It Right Once

Carpe Diem at its finest. Do It Right Once is one of Akeda's earliest hits, and preaches the importance of living life to the fullest, as there is only one opportunity to do so.
